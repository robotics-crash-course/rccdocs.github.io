Raft v0.6 Pinout for Winter 2024

IMPORTANT:

  • Make sure BOARD is OFF when plugging things in
  • Please ask for help BEFORE powering if you are not 100% confident with the wiring
  • SHUNTS connect green and white pins
  • If 5V devices are not working, make sure using a v0.6 Raft

+5V Devices

Servo Motor

  • SHUNT on Pin 16
  • orange wire to *16
  • red wire to +5V
  • brown wire to GND

Left Encoder

  • SHUNTS on Pins 18 & 19
  • Black wire to GND
  • Red wire to +5V
  • Green wire to *18
  • Blue wire to *19

Right Encoder

  • SHUNTS on Pins 20 & 21
  • Black wire to GND
  • Red wire to +5V
  • Green wire to *20
  • Blue wire to *21

I2C Devices

SHUNTS on BOTH Pin 14 & Pin 15
note: *I2C1 Bus has *SCL and *SDA
another note: wire color does not matter

Lidar (VL53L0X)

  • VCC to 3V3
  • GND to GND
  • SCL to *SCL
  • SDA to *SDA
  • note: other 2 pins on lidar are unconnected

IMU (MPU6050)

  • VCC to 3V3
  • GND to GND
  • SCL to *SCL
  • SDA to *SDA
  • note: other 4 pins on imu are unconnected

Motor Controller

If not done so already, remove shunts from ENA and ENB

Power

  • Black wire from GND on Raft to GND on motor controller
  • Red wire from +Vin on Raft to +12V on motor controller

Left Motor

  • Black wire to OUT1
  • Red wire to OUT2

Right Motor

  • Black wire to OUT4
  • Red wire to OUT3

Control Signals (all to GPIO Pins)

  • ENA to Pin 7
  • IN1 to Pin 8
  • IN2 to Pin 9
  • IN3 to Pin 10
  • IN4 to Pin 11
  • ENB to Pin 12
